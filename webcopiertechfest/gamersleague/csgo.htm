Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Cash Prizes: 2 Lakhs

Register Is Now Closed

Techfest summons Gamers from all over India for this Huge Tournament who will battle it out for a huge Prize Pool of 2 Lakhs for CS:GO.
The event consists of both online qualifiers and LAN Playoffs. All Teams will play knockout matches online (Bo1) and will progress to further rounds. Top 8 teams will then be invited for the LAN PLayoffs at Techfest, IIT Bombay during 29th-31st December. Semi-Finals and Finals will both be Bo3.
* No fees for Registration *
The Online Qualifiers will be on Playtonia Servers

Format:

  • Maps: Standard Valve Active Duty Maps
  • Gameplay: Standard Competitive Gameplay
  • Format: Semi-Finals and Finals will be Bo3, rest Bo1
  • Venue: Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay- 400076
