RITZY

FASHION IS NOT NECESSARILY ABOUT LABELS. IT'S NOT BRANDS.
IT'S ABOUT SOMETHING ELSE THAT COMES FROM WITHIN YOU.

VEDA18 presents Ritzy Mr. And Miss VEDA. If your dream is to ooze glamour and confidence, come join the parade and win exciting prizes. Be part of the extravaganza gala and we promise you a night where your wings will soar.

Fashion Talks

The competition consists of four rounds:
1.The first round will be the ethnic (Traditional) round.
2.The second round will be the Casual and Introduction round.
3.The third round will be the Designer wear round.
4.The fourth round will be the Formal evening wear.

The best of the talents will be rewarded with cash prizes along with gift hampers.
