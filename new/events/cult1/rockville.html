WHY MUSIC? WHY BAND?
Music for the sake of music. "Any responsible music educator will emphatically echo this important mantra; it is the keystone theme that serves as the foundation of music teaching, music learning, and music performance. There is no substitute or replacement for music making as it relates to the positive growth and development of the human mind, body, and spirit. Participation in band avails the musician to the infinite journey of creative expression connecting to a language (music) that is understood, communicated, and appreciated by all of mankind around the globe. More importantly, MUSIC IS A PLACE FOR EVERYONE."   -Tim Lautzenheiser

Battle Of Bands

Battle of the Bands is the annual band competition of VEDA. Since its inception, it has been growing every year, with last year seeing a footfall of over 2000.

The competition consists of two rounds: The Preliminaries and The Finals. The Prelims would be conducted on 2nd of April, while the finals on 3rd.

To reward the best of the talents we have a prize pool of 80kINR. "It all comes down to the same thing: Extremely good music, a great team and a lot of luck". Rulebook
